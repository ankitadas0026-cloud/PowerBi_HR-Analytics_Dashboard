	HR Analytics Dashboard – Objectives
•	Provide a clear and consolidated view of employee attrition and workforce trends
•	Enable HR and management teams to monitor overall employee strength and attrition levels
•	Highlight key HR KPIs such as total employees, attrition count, attrition rate, average age, average salary, and average tenure

Analyze attrition patterns across different demographics including:
o	Gender
o	Age groups
o	Education background
•	Identify salary slabs with higher attrition to assess compensation-related risks
•	Examine attrition trends based on years at the company to understand early and late-stage employee exits
•	Provide job-role–wise attrition analysis to identify high-risk roles
•	Evaluate job satisfaction levels across different roles and satisfaction ratings

	Support data-driven HR decisions related to:
o	Employee retention strategies
o	Compensation planning
o	Workforce planning
o	Talent management

•	Enable interactive analysis through filters for department selection
•	Help HR leaders uncover hidden attrition drivers and improve employee engagement, retention, and organizational performance
